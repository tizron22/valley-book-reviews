About Us

What is Valley Book Reviews?

Valley Books Review is a site that has been designed around bringing people together who love reading, by writing reviews this allows you to tell your story on how you found the book which will give others insight into if they maybe interesting in reading it.

By writing your review this will allow others to either like or dislike it based on mulitple factors, do they agree with your reivew or do they dislike the book as a whole. Users of the site will be able to see which books or reviews are popular from a glance thanks to our voting system, we allow users to either like or dislike each review with the ability to change this later down the line should they wish.

To be able to take full advantage of our site, please register for an account this can be done at any time by clicking the icon on the top right of the site and click on the register option. Once you have registered and logged in you will be able to start making your own reviews or voting on your favourite reviews from other creators.

Our aim to connect people all over the world who share the passsion of reading.
